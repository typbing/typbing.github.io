About Me

Hello! I’m Binghui Li, a recent graduate with a Master’s in Geographic Information Science from Clark University. I’m passionate about transforming spatial data into actionable insights that support planning, communication, and decision-making.

During my internship at the City of West Jordan, I developed and managed a variety of geospatial projects across public safety, utilities, and urban development. I built interactive tools like the Fire Incident ArcGIS Dashboard, created static cartographic products such as the 2025 Voting Precincts Map and Fire Hydrant Zones, and supported infrastructure monitoring with tools like the Drone Mapping project.

My work demonstrates proficiency in ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS Online, ArcGIS Dashboard, Web AppBuilder, and web mapping technologies like Leaflet and ArcGIS Maps SDK for JavaScript. I also leverage skills in data visualization, cartographic design, field data collection using Field Map, and am proficient in using various equipment such as plotters, printers, scanners, and more.

Whether it's building interactive dashboards, publishing polished static maps, or using drones to visualize change over time, I strive to communicate spatial stories clearly and effectively. Explore my featured work including the Solar Panel Visualization, Housing Mortgage Map, and Construction Projects StoryMap.

I’m currently seeking opportunities where I can contribute my geospatial expertise and storytelling skills to impactful, real-world projects.
