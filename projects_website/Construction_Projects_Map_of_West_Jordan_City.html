Construction Projects in West Jordan City

Project Description

This ArcGIS StoryMap provides an overview of both ongoing and completed construction projects in West Jordan City. It is intended to inform residents, city planners, and stakeholders about infrastructure improvements, current development activities, and finished works across the city.

Key Features:

  • Interactive maps with project locations and descriptions.
  • Images and timelines of both active and completed projects.
  • User-friendly storytelling interface.

Skills Used: ArcGIS StoryMaps, Public Communication, Infrastructure Mapping, Urban Planning
