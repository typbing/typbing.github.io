TransEcon: Transportation and Economic Analysis in NYC

Project Description

TransEcon investigates the influence of transportation infrastructure on economic development in New York City. The project examines correlations between transportation access and various economic indicators, aiming to address key questions about the socioeconomic impacts of transportation infrastructure in NYC.

Key Features:

  • Analysis of how transportation access influences economic growth, property values, and business activity in NYC.
  • Assessment of the equitable distribution of economic benefits from transportation infrastructure across different demographic groups.

Skills Used: PostgreSQL, Data Analysis, Socioeconomic Research
